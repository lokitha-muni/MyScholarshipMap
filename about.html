About MyScholarshipMap

Learn about our mission to make education accessible to all Indian students

Our Mission

MyScholarshipMap was created with a simple mission: to help Indian students find and apply for scholarships that match their unique profiles.

We believe that financial constraints should never prevent talented students from pursuing their educational dreams. By providing a comprehensive database of scholarships and resources, we aim to bridge the gap between students and financial support opportunities.

Our platform is designed to simplify the scholarship search process by allowing students to filter opportunities based on their gender, caste, income level, and state of residence. This personalized approach ensures that students only see scholarships for which they are eligible, saving them time and increasing their chances of success.

Have Suggestions or Feedback?

We're constantly working to improve MyScholarshipMap. If you have suggestions, feedback, or know of scholarships we should add, we'd love to hear from you!
